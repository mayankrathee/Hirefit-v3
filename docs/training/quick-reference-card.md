## Create a Job

1. Jobs → New Job
2. Fill: Title, Description, Requirements
3. Save (Draft) or Publish (Active)

**Tip**: List specific skills in Requirements field

---

## Upload Resumes

1. Go to a job
2. Click "Upload Resumes"
3. Drag & drop PDFs or Word docs
4. Wait for AI score (~30 sec)

**Supported**: PDF, DOC, DOCX (max 10MB)

---

## AI Scores

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 80+ | Excellent - Interview now |
| 60-79 | Good - Strong candidate |
| 40-59 | Fair - Review manually |
| < 40 | Weak - May not fit |

---

## Team Invitations

1. Settings → Team → Invite
2. Enter email + role
3. They receive email link
4. They click to join

**Roles**: Admin, HR Admin, Recruiter, Hiring Manager, Viewer
